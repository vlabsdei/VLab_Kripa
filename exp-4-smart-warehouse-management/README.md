# Smart Warehouse Management Simulator

## Overview

The Smart Warehouse Management Simulator is an Industry 4.0 virtual laboratory designed to demonstrate modern warehouse operations, inventory management, automated material handling, and logistics optimization. The simulator enables learners to study how storage capacity, incoming orders, robot performance, and inventory thresholds affect overall warehouse efficiency.

The experiment provides a digital representation of a smart warehouse environment where users can configure operational parameters and analyze warehouse performance using real-time Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).

---

## Objectives

- Understand the fundamentals of smart warehouse management.
- Analyze storage utilization and inventory allocation.
- Study the impact of incoming orders on warehouse performance.
- Evaluate Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) utilization.
- Monitor warehouse congestion and operational efficiency.
- Understand Industry 4.0 applications in warehouse automation.
- Interpret warehouse KPIs for decision-making.

## Input Parameters

### Storage Capacity

Represents the total storage space available within the warehouse.

### Incoming Orders

Represents the volume of customer orders entering the warehouse system.

### Robot Speed

Represents the operating speed of the warehouse AGV.

### Inventory Threshold

Represents the minimum inventory level before replenishment actions are triggered.

---

## Output Parameters

### Storage Utilization

Percentage of warehouse capacity currently occupied.

### Order Processing Time

Time required to process and dispatch incoming orders.

### Robot Utilization

Percentage of AGV operational usage.

### Warehouse Efficiency

Overall operational performance of the warehouse.

### Congestion Index

Indicator of warehouse congestion risk.

### Inventory Health

Represents inventory stability and stock availability.
