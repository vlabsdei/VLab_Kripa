### About the Experiment

The Smart Warehouse Management Simulator is a virtual laboratory experiment developed under the SmartFactory 4.0 Virtual Lab project. This experiment helps students understand the working principles of smart warehouse operations, inventory management, automated material handling, and logistics optimization used in modern Industry 4.0 environments.

The experiment provides an interactive simulation where users can modify warehouse operating parameters and observe their impact on storage utilization, AGV performance, order processing efficiency, congestion levels, and inventory health through a real-time digital twin visualization.
