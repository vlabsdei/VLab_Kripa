### About the Experiment

The AI Quality Inspection System is a virtual laboratory experiment developed under the SmartFactory 4.0 Virtual Lab project. This experiment introduces students to the principles of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Computer Vision, and Machine Vision used for automated quality inspection in modern Industry 4.0 manufacturing environments.

The experiment provides an interactive simulation where users can configure inspection parameters such as product size, defect threshold, camera accuracy, inspection speed, and lighting conditions to analyze their impact on defect detection, AI confidence score, inspection accuracy, product acceptance, and inspection throughput. Students gain practical knowledge of intelligent visual inspection, automated defect detection, AI-assisted decision-making, and industrial quality assurance techniques widely used in smart manufacturing systems.
