### About the Experiment

The Digital Twin Factory Simulator is a virtual laboratory experiment developed under the SmartFactory 4.0 Virtual Lab project. This experiment introduces students to the concept of Digital Twin technology used in Industry 4.0 smart manufacturing systems.

The experiment provides an interactive simulation where users can configure factory operating parameters and observe their impact on machine utilization, production efficiency, warehouse performance, inventory management, power consumption, and operational analytics through a real-time Digital Twin visualization. Students gain practical knowledge of how Digital Twins enable intelligent monitoring, predictive analysis, and data-driven decision-making in modern manufacturing environments.
