# References

1. **Mobley, R. K. (2002).** *An Introduction to Predictive Maintenance* (2nd ed.). Butterworth-Heinemann.
   - A fundamental guide on the principles and applications of predictive maintenance in industrial environments.

2. **Hashemian, H. M. (2010).** *State-of-the-Art Predictive Maintenance Techniques*. IEEE Transactions on Instrumentation and Measurement, 60(1), 226-236.
   - Details modern sensing and analysis techniques for predicting equipment failures.

3. **Wang, J., et al. (2017).** *A Review of Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) and Smart Factory*. IEEE Access.
   - Explores the role of IoT sensors and real-time data in Industry 4.0 manufacturing.

4. **Lee, J., Kao, H. A., & Yang, S. (2014).** *Service Innovation and Smart Analytics for Industry 4.0 and Big Data Environment*. Procedia CIRP, 16, 3-8.
   - Discusses how big data analytics and condition monitoring drive predictive maintenance strategies.

5. **ISO 13374 (Condition Monitoring and Diagnostics of Machines).**
   - International standard detailing data processing and information flow in condition monitoring systems.
